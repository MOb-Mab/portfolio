AED rental service

Project Overview

This project involved designing a platform to help users locate and access Automated External Defibrillator (AED) devices in emergency situations. I worked on both web and mobile prototypes, creating user-friendly interfaces in Figma that focused on clear navigation and quick access to essential information. The design applied basic cross-platform principles to ensure consistency and ease of use across devices.
